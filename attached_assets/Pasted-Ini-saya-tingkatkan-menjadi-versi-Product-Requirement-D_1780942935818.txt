==================================================
VISION
======

MaxiChat tidak hanya menjadi Omnichannel.

MaxiChat harus berevolusi menjadi:

Omnichannel
+
AI
+
CRM
+
AI Sales Assistant

Tujuan utama:

1. Mengurangi lead hilang
2. Mengurangi lupa follow up
3. Membantu sales closing lebih cepat
4. Memberikan pipeline penjualan visual
5. Memberikan insight penjualan berbasis AI
6. Menjadi alasan utama customer upgrade ke Enterprise

==================================================
CRM ARCHITECTURE
================

CRM HARUS MENYATU DENGAN CHAT.

JANGAN MEMBUAT CRM TERPISAH YANG KOMPLEKS.

Inspirasi:

* Trello
* Pipedrive
* Monday
* ClickUp

CRM harus ringan dan mudah dipahami.

==================================================
CRM STAGES
==========

Default:

1. New Lead
2. Inquiry
3. Quotation Sent
4. Follow Up
5. Negotiation
6. Won
7. Lost

Tenant dapat:

* menambah stage
* menghapus stage
* mengubah urutan stage

Drag and Drop wajib tersedia.

==================================================
OPPORTUNITY DETECTION ENGINE
============================

AI harus membaca percakapan secara otomatis.

Deteksi:

* harga
* quotation
* penawaran
* katalog
* spesifikasi
* stok
* demo
* produk
* mesin
* pembelian
* investasi
* budget

AI harus menghasilkan:

Lead Score

0 - 100

Kategori:

0-39 = Low
40-69 = Medium
70-100 = High

==================================================
LEAD SCORING FACTORS
====================

Contoh sinyal positif:

* meminta harga
* meminta quotation
* meminta katalog
* menyebut budget
* menyebut perusahaan
* meminta demo
* bertanya stok

Contoh sinyal negatif:

* tidak tertarik
* hanya survei
* salah sambung
* tidak ada kebutuhan

AI harus menjelaskan alasan skor.

==================================================
TOGGLE 1
AUTO CREATE CRM OPPORTUNITY
===========================

Lokasi:

Sidebar kanan percakapan.

OFF / ON

Jika ON:

Lead score >= threshold

otomatis membuat opportunity.

Threshold dapat diubah tenant.

Default:

70

Jika OFF:

AI hanya memberikan rekomendasi.

==================================================
CRM OPPORTUNITY DATA
====================

AI boleh mengisi otomatis:

* Customer Name
* WhatsApp Number
* Company Name
* Product Interest
* Lead Score
* Estimated Value
* Notes
* Source Channel
* Owner
* Last Activity

Semua field harus editable.

WAITING STATUS ENGINE
=====================

AI harus menentukan:

WAITING CUSTOMER

==================================================
TOGGLE 2
AUTO FOLLOW UP
==============

Sidebar kanan.

OFF / ON

Jika OFF:

AI hanya memberi rekomendasi.

Tidak mengirim pesan.

---

Jika ON:

AI dapat membuat follow up otomatis.

==================================================
FOLLOW UP TIMING
================

Pilihan:

24 jam

48 jam

72 jam

7 hari

Custom

AI harus menghitung berdasarkan:

Last Meaningful Interaction

Bukan timestamp terakhir.

==================================================
LAST MEANINGFUL INTERACTION
===========================

Contoh:

Customer:

"Baik pak saya pelajari dulu"

↓

Meaningful Interaction

---

Customer:

"ok"

Tidak selalu meaningful.

AI harus memahami konteks.

==================================================
FOLLOW UP SEQUENCE
==================

Maksimum:

Follow Up 1

Follow Up 2

Follow Up 3

Setelah Follow Up 3:

STOP

Tidak boleh spam.

==================================================
FOLLOW UP RULES
===============

JANGAN FOLLOW UP JIKA:

* customer membalas
* opportunity Won
* opportunity Lost
* customer meminta stop kontak
* customer meminta unsubscribe
* customer meminta tidak dihubungi
* sales memiliki tugas yang belum selesai

==================================================
FOLLOW UP MESSAGE GENERATOR

Mempertimbangkan:

* produk

* nama customer
* stage
* perusahaan

menggunakan template generik yang sama.

PERMISSIONS
===========

==================================================
MULTI TENANT
============

Semua data harus terisolasi.

Tidak boleh ada data lintas tenant.

Semua query wajib tenant-scoped.

==================================================
SCALABILITY
===========

Desain harus mampu menangani:

* 10.000+ tenant
* 1.000.000+ opportunity
* 100.000.000+ message

Gunakan arsitektur queue untuk:

* AI analysis
* follow up scheduler
* revenue forecast

* revenue forecast error
* pipeline inconsistency

* multi tenant leakage
